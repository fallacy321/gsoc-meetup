About GSoC Meetup 2015

GSoC MeetUp 2015 aims to help the students in providing impact of open source world and its importance in our life, followed by the experience with open source organizations.

GSoC students/mentors/administrators would be giving a talk about their experience with their organizations.

Everybody is welcome to attend this meetup.

Schedule
At the end, there will be a Q&A round for an interactive session.

The main purpose for holding this event is to gather present and past gsocers at a single location along with other open source enthusiasts and offer an opportunity to share our experiences with each other.
